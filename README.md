# Mushee Yield: Powered by Starkzap

**Built by Mushee (UK incorporated)**  
**Tagline:** Make yield feel like a button.

## Overview

Mushee Yield is our attempt to make staking and yield feel simple, clean, and human.

We built this as a Starkzap-powered demo to show that onchain experiences do not have to feel overwhelming or overly technical. Instead of throwing users into a crowded DeFi interface, we focused on a smoother journey: connect a wallet, fund on testnet, discover pools, stake, track positions, and claim rewards in a way that feels approachable.

At the heart of the product is a very simple loop:

**connect → get testnet funds → load pools → stake → track → claim**

As we kept building, the project became more than just a staking demo. It turned into a broader product playground for exploring how crypto apps can feel more consumer-friendly, more playful, and more welcoming to everyday users.

That is why Mushee Yield also includes extra product surfaces like Bitcoin Birthday Cards, a Spin the Wheel reward demo, and a Trading Hub concept.

## What We Built

Mushee Yield includes:

- **Cartridge wallet onboarding** through Starkzap
- **General wallet connect flow** for a more flexible testnet experience
- **Validator presets** and pool discovery
- **Pool metrics cards** for clearer staking information
- **Staking flow** with dashboard tracking and reward claims
- **Faucet section** for Starknet Sepolia testnet funding
- **Bitcoin Birthday Cards** prototype
- **Spin the Wheel** reward demo surface
- **Trading Hub** concept for exploring trader profiles and copy-style actions
- **Ready Wallet** section
- **Useful links section** for the live demo and repository

## Why We Built It

We started with one simple thought:

**What if yield felt as easy as pressing a button?**

A lot of crypto products are powerful, but they can still feel intimidating for normal users. Even simple actions can feel heavier than they need to be. We wanted to experiment with a product experience that feels lighter, cleaner, and easier to understand.

Mushee Yield is our way of exploring that idea.

It is part staking demo, part UX experiment, and part product concept for a future where crypto apps feel less like technical dashboards and more like polished consumer products.

## Why Starkzap

Starkzap gave us the foundation we needed to shape a real staking flow on Starknet.

It helped us structure the experience around wallet onboarding, validator selection, pool discovery, staking, and reward-related actions. That gave us room to focus not only on what the product does, but also on how it feels to use.

For us, Starkzap was valuable because it made it possible to prototype a more usable and approachable staking experience without losing the core onchain flow.

## Product Direction

Mushee Yield is built around a simple belief:

**crypto should feel usable, not intimidating.**

We wanted the app to feel soft, clear, and modern instead of cluttered or overly technical. The goal was not just to make something functional, but to make something people would actually enjoy clicking through.

The wider product direction is centered around:

- simple onboarding
- lighter user flows
- clean, direct actions
- playful reward surfaces
- more approachable DeFi UX
- a more consumer-friendly feel overall

## Core User Flow

The main loop in Mushee Yield is:

1. **Connect wallet**
2. **Get testnet funds**
3. **Load validator pools**
4. **Choose where to stake**
5. **Stake into a pool**
6. **Track dashboard state**
7. **Claim rewards**

We wanted this flow to feel straightforward and easy to follow, especially on testnet.

## Feature Highlights

### Staking and Yield

The core of the product is a simpler staking flow. Users can connect, discover pools, choose a validator path, and move through staking actions without feeling buried in too much information.

### Pool Discovery

We included validator presets and pool discovery to reduce friction and make it easier for users to move from curiosity to action.

### Starknet Sepolia Faucet

The faucet section helps users get testnet funds quickly so they can actually try the experience instead of getting stuck before they begin.

### Bitcoin Birthday Cards

This is a playful prototype that explores how gifting-style crypto experiences could sit alongside yield and wallet interactions in a more consumer-facing app.

### Spin the Wheel

The spin surface adds a lighter, more interactive reward layer to the experience. It helps the app feel more engaging and less purely transactional.

### Trading Hub

The Trading Hub explores a social trading direction, where users can view trader profiles, check activity, discover strategies, and imagine copy-style actions in a more visually rich and product-focused environment.

## Design Approach

We kept the visual style bright, clean, and lightweight.

The white and orange/gold palette gives the app a warmer and more inviting feel, which was important to us. A lot of crypto products lean dark, dense, and technical. We wanted this to feel different.

The design focus was on:

- generous spacing
- clean cards and panels
- rounded layouts
- clear call-to-actions
- simple copy
- a more seamless product feel

We wanted the interface to feel modern, premium, and easy to move through.
